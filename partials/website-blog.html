Personal Blog

Gentle x Serene x Sincere

Overview

A personal blog website designed for Karina Paz, built on the Wix platform. The site uses soft visuals and warm tones to express her faith reflections and life insights, creating a sincere and peaceful reading atmosphere.

Concept

  • Soft palette x generous whitespace: Evokes serenity and a sense of spiritual calm.
  • Clear navigation structure: Helps readers focus on words and inner reflection.
  • Handwritten typography x natural imagery: Conveys authenticity and warmth.
