Dragon Boat Festival

Watercolor x Illustrative x Cultural Festivity

Overview

A poster designed for the Taiwanese Society of Oklahoma’s Dragon Boat Festival event. The theme blends “talent showcase x traditional celebration ,” illustrated in a playful, child-like style that captures the festive spirit and cultural gathering of the Dragon Boat Festival. The composition centers on a bamboo steamer filled with rice dumplings, reimagined as a stage—symbolizing inclusiveness, unity, and the passing on of cultural heritage.

Concept

  • Bamboo steamer as stage: Symbolizes unity, celebration, and cultural connection.
  • Soft watercolor palette: Brings a festive, summery, and gentle mood.
  • Playful crowd illustration: Highlights joyful participation across generations.
