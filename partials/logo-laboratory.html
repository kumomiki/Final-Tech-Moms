Lin Lab Logo

Minimal x Geometric x Scientific

Overview

A visual identity designed for a biomedical research lab. The logo integrates elements of the human brain, DNA, and cell, symbolizing the lab’s focus on neuroscience and molecular medicine.

Concept

  • Human profile × DNA helix: Represents the connection between mechanism and medicine.
  • Gradient blue tone: Conveys professionalism, intelligence, and scientific precision.
  • Circular composition: Suggests unity, innovation, and continuous discovery.
