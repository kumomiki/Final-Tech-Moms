Wedding Invitation Card

Gold geometry x blush watercolor — elegant & modern.

Overview

Designed with an elegant, modern aesthetic that evokes a sense of happiness, it combines golden geometric heart motifs with soft watercolor touches to convey a feeling of “preciousness and romance.”

Concept

  • The main color palette features Gold & Blush — gold symbolizes eternity, while blush creates a soft, romantic ambiance.
  • A mix of handwritten and formal typefaces balances personal warmth with timeless elegance and structure.
  • The layout features a clear structure that highlights information hierarchy and creates a smooth reading rhythm.
